Dr. Devi Prasad Shetty
Dr. Devi Prasad Shetty- as Bypasswale Baba, i.e. the Saint who Grants Bypasses, is treating a young patient.
  • Here is a timeline of Dr. Shetty's life:
  • 1953 - Born at village Dakshina Kannada district, Karnataka, India. The eighth of nine children.
  • 1964 - Decided to become a heart surgeon when he was in fifth grade at school after hearing about Dr. Christiaan Barnard, a South African surgeon who had just performed the world's first heart transplant.
  • 1979 - Completing his Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery(MBBS) in 1979.
  • 2000 - Founded Rabindranath Tagore International Institute of Cardiac Sciences (RTIICS) in Mukundapur, Kolkata, West Bengal.
  • 2001 - Founded Narayana Hrudayalaya (NH), a multi-specialty hospital in Bommasandra on the outskirts of Bangalore.
  • 2004 - Awarded the awarded the Padma Shri,is the fourth highest civilian award in the Republic of India.
  • 2005 - Won the Schwab Foundation's award in 2005
  • 2011 - Won the 2011 The Economist Innovation Awards for the Business process field.
  • 2011 - Honorary Degree, University of Minnesota.
  • 2012 - Agreement with TriMedx, a subsidiary of Ascension Health, to create a joint venture for a chain of hospitals in India.
  • 2012 - Awarded chief patron of Indian Association of Clinical Cardiologists.
  • 2013 - Awarded the awarded the Padma Bhusha, is the third highest civilian award in the Republic of India.
